05 — Common questions

Things people ask before they sign up.

What is a "PM OS" exactly?
It's a set of AI skills that live on your laptop and know your business. Think of it like having a product management consultant on call 24/7, except it already knows your customers, your ideas, and your decisions. You ask it a question, it gives you an answer built on everything it's learned about your business. And it gets smarter every time you use it.
Do I need to know how to code?
No. Zero coding. You'll be typing plain English into Claude, describing your business, asking questions, building skills. If you can write an email, you can build your PM OS.
What's a "skill" in this context?
A skill is a reusable AI prompt that knows your business. Instead of starting a new conversation with Claude every time, you run a skill (like "customer research" or "idea validation") and it already has all your context. You build it once, and it works forever.
I'm already a product manager. Is this for me?
Yes, if you want to become AI-enhanced. You'll build a system that makes you faster at the PM work you already do: customer research, idea validation, prioritization, positioning. Same frameworks, but now you have an AI partner that remembers everything and does the structured thinking in seconds.
What do I need before the workshop?
A laptop and a Claude Pro subscription ($20/mo). We walk you through setup in the first 30 minutes. You also need a real business or serious idea to work with. The workshop is hands-on with your actual context, not hypotheticals.
What do I walk away with?
A working system on your laptop: your business context file, four core AI skills (Customer Research, Idea Validation, Prioritization, Positioning), three utility skills (Status, Update, Reflect), and the knowledge to build new skills on your own. Plus a Slack community with your cohort.
Will this still work after the workshop?
That's the whole point. Your PM OS keeps working. Tuesday, next month, next year. Every time you use it, it gets smarter. And because we teach you how to build new skills, you can extend it as your business grows.
Is this virtual or in-person?
Virtual. You'll be on Zoom, screen-sharing, building in real time. Small group (15 max) so you get real attention, not a webinar you watch passively.
